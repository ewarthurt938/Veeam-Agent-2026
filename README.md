## ❓ Frequently asked questions

How often should I back up my data?
Most users back up their computer once every day. This keeps your data fresh and lowers the risk of losing new work.

Can I turn off my computer during a backup?
Avoid turning off your computer while a task runs. If you must stop the computer, the software pauses the task and resumes it when you turn the machine back on.

Do I need an internet connection for backups?
You do not need the internet if you save your backups to an external USB drive connected to your computer. You only need the internet if you save backups to a location outside your home or office.

What if my computer uses a lot of power?
The software includes a setting to limit resources. This ensures your computer remains fast while the backup runs in the background.

Where do I find logs if an error happens?
The software saves logs in the history section. You can view these logs to see exactly what happened during a failed attempt. If a problem persists, use these logs to explain the situation to someone who can help you resolve it.

Does this software work on Windows 7?
No. This version works only with Windows 10 and 11 to ensure high performance and better security.

How do I remove the software?
Open your Windows Control Panel, select Uninstall a Program, find the Veeam Agent in the list, and click Uninstall. Follow the prompts to remove the program and its settings from your computer.
